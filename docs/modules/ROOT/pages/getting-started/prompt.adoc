** Objective
*** Convert the given article into structured, self-paced learning content that a learner can follow independently

** Instructions
*** Identify the main concepts, supporting ideas, and practical insights
*** Transform the content into a guided learning format
*** Ensure the learner can understand, apply, and practice the topic step by step

** Output Structure

*** Overall Summary
**** Provide a single-line explanation of the entire concept

*** Learning Objectives
**** List 5–7 clear outcomes the learner will achieve
**** Each objective should start with an action verb (Understand, Design, Build, Implement, Create, Develop)

*** Structured Concept Breakdown
**** Group related ideas into named topic areas
**** Use the hierarchy:
***** `*` Topic area title
***** `**` Sub-topic
***** `***` Explanation or detail point
**** Each topic area should contain 2–4 sub-topics
**** Each sub-topic should have 1–3 short explanation points
**** Remove filler and repetition
**** Preserve useful examples, analogies, and distinctions

*** Step-by-Step Learning Flow
**** Organise into exactly 4 steps:
***** Step 1: Foundation concept
***** Step 2: Core idea
***** Step 3: Advanced understanding
***** Step 4: Real-world application
**** Each step should have 2–3 actionable sub-points

*** Quick Revision Points
**** Summarise 7–10 key takeaways
**** Each point should be one short, memorable sentence
**** Use the pattern: subject + action/insight
***** Example: "Good prompts = clear structure + context + role"

** Formatting Rules
*** Use AsciiDoc-style bullet hierarchy only:
**** `*` for section titles and top-level topic areas
**** `**` for sub-sections, topic areas, and content items
**** `***` for sub-topics and step details
**** `****` for explanations and detail points
*** Section titles (Overall Summary, Learning Objectives, etc.) appear at the `*` level
*** Keep sentences short and simple — one idea per line
*** Do NOT use markdown headings (`#`, `##`, `###`)
*** Do NOT use bold, italic, or inline formatting
*** Focus on clarity and usability for self-paced learning
*** Ensure content is actionable, not just descriptive

** Output Requirements
*** Output only bullet-structured content using the hierarchy above
*** Ensure it is clean and ready to paste into AsciiDoc notes or documentation tools
*** No introductory or closing commentary — just the structured content
