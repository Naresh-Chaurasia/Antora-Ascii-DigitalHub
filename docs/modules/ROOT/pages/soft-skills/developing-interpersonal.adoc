== Module 1

=== Let's begin with the first topic

* Communication style self-awareness
  ** Identify your own communication style
  *** Reflect on how you usually speak, listen, and respond
  ** Recognize different communication preferences
  *** People vary in how they express and receive information

* Types of communication behavior
  ** Expressive vs reserved communication
  *** Some people are more vocal and leading
  *** Others prefer listening and supporting
  ** Analytical vs reactive approach
  *** Some analyze and ask questions before responding
  *** Others share opinions quickly

* Importance of effective communication
  ** Communication can be ambiguous
  *** What is said may differ from what is understood
  ** Influencing factors
  *** Perceptions affect interpretation
  *** Biases shape understanding
  *** Reactions impact outcomes

* #Adapting communication style#
  ** No need to change your natural style
  *** Maintain authenticity
  ** Adjust based on the other person
  *** Modify what you say
  *** Modify how you say it
  ** Prevent misunderstandings
  *** Avoid communication clashes

* Benefits of understanding communication styles
  ** Improves clarity and effectiveness
  *** Reduces confusion in conversations
  ** Enhances connection with others
  *** Builds better relationships
  ** Acts like rules of the road analogy
  *** Know when to lead, follow, or adjust
  *** Helps navigate conversations smoothly

* Example of effective communication
  ** Ginny Rometty’s communication style
  *** Strong and impactful speaker
  *** Connects through eye contact
  *** Inspires people to follow her lead
