* *Ask questions on Word documents*

** Summarize this document in 5 bullets and call out any decisions and action items.
** What are the key risks mentioned, and where (which section) are they discussed?
** Create a 10-question FAQ based on this document, with short answers.

* *Revise edits to your Word documents*

** Suggest improvements for clarity and structure across the document, then provide a revised version of the introduction.
** Identify repetition and rewrite to remove redundancy while keeping all key details.
** Proofread for grammar and tighten sentences—don’t change technical terms.

** Create meeting minutes from this content with sections: Attendees, Agenda, Discussion, Decisions, Action items (Owner + Due).
** Generate a one-page executive summary plus a slide-style outline (8 slide titles with 3 bullets each).
** Create a RACI matrix from this text and present it as a table.

== Excel


* *Data – General Analysis*

** Analyse this dataset and summarise key trends, anomalies, and insights in bullet points.
** Give me a high-level business interpretation of this data as if presenting to leadership.
** Identify patterns, correlations, or outliers that I should be concerned about.

* *Data – Q\&A*

** What is the total and average [sales/revenue/cost] by [region/product/month]?
** Which [category/customer/product] is performing the best and worst, and why?
** Answer in plain English: what story does this data tell?

* *Data Filtering – 1 (Basic)*

** Filter the data to show only records where [condition].
** Show me rows from [date range] with [status = X].
** Keep only the top 10 records based on [metric].

* *Data Filtering – 2 (Advanced)*

** Filter the data to include [Condition A] AND [Condition B], but exclude [Condition C].
** Create a filtered view showing only high-risk or low-performing entries.
** Highlight rows that meet multiple conditions.

* *Data Formatting*

** Format this data as a clean, presentation-ready table.
** Apply conditional formatting to highlight high, medium, and low values.
** Standardise date, currency, and percentage formats across the sheet.

* *Data Manipulation*

** Create a new calculated column for [metric] using existing columns.
** Split this column into multiple columns based on [delimiter or logic].
** Merge these two datasets and remove duplicates.

* *Data Sorting*

** Sort this data by [metric] in descending order.
** Sort first by [Region], then by [Revenue].
** Rank the records from highest to lowest performance.

* *Data Visualisation*

** Create a chart that best represents this data for business review.
** Visualise trends over time using an appropriate chart.
** Create a dashboard-style summary with 2–3 key visuals.

* *Copilot – General Use Cases*

** Explain what insights a business user should focus on from this sheet.
** Summarise this workbook in 5 executive-level bullet points.
** Suggest next actions based on the data.
