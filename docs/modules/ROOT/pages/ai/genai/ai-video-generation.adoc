== Module 1


=== Overview of HeyGen & Trupeer

* Haygen overview
** AI tool for creating digital human avatars. Avatars can present, interact, and speak like real humans

* Haygen features
** Realistic avatars with accurate lip-sync
*** Supports multiple languages
** Voice options available
*** Clone your own voice or use AI-generated voices
** High customization flexibility
*** Change backgrounds, avatars, and templates

* Haygen value proposition
** Enables fast video production
*** #Eliminates need for camera presence#


* Troupier overview
** AI tool for product video and guide creation
*** Focuses on automation of video generation

* Troupier features
** Combines multiple videos into one output
*** Simplifies content assembly process
** No manual editing required
*** Videos are ready for publishing
** Supports custom voice and avatars
*** Personalizes generated content
