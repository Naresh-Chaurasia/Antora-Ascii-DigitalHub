= Case Study: Understanding Agentic AI with Stock Investment Example
:toc: right

== Scenario

You have $100 and want to invest it in the US stock market.

Instead of figuring everything out manually, you ask an Agentic AI system:

"How should I invest my $100 in the US stock market?"

== Goal

The main goal is:

* To get the best possible investment advice for $100

== How Agentic AI Approaches the Problem

=== Step 1: Goal Understanding

The AI clearly understands the goal:

* Provide the best investment strategy for $100

Unlike traditional systems, it focuses on *what needs to be achieved*, not just *what task to perform*.

=== Step 2: Planning Actions

The AI creates a plan of actions to achieve the goal. For example:

* Analyze top companies in the US stock market
* Study current financial conditions (interest rates, inflation, etc.)
* Evaluate global market trends
* Assess risk vs return for small investments

This step is important because the AI decides *what steps are needed* without human guidance.

=== Step 3: Executing Actions

The AI then performs the planned actions:

* Collects stock data
* Evaluates company performance
* Analyzes market trends
* Compares different investment options

All of this happens:

* With minimal human intervention
* Automatically and continuously

=== Step 4: Producing the Outcome

Based on its analysis, the AI provides:

* Recommended stocks or ETFs
* Suggested allocation of $100
* Reasoning behind the choices

== Key Insight

The entire process:

* Does not require step-by-step human instructions
* Is handled independently by the AI system

This is what makes it *agentic*.

== Definition of Agentic AI

Agentic AI is a system that can:

* Take or define a goal
* Plan actions to achieve that goal
* Execute those actions
* Do all of this with minimal or no human intervention

In simple words:

*Agentic AI can think, act, and improve on its own.*

== Key Characteristics of Agentic AI

=== 1. Autonomy

* Works independently
* Requires little to no human involvement

=== 2. Goal-Oriented

* Focuses on achieving a goal
* Not limited to predefined tasks

=== 3. Adaptability

* Learns from outcomes
* Adjusts actions based on environment and results

== Summary

In this case study:

* Goal → Invest $100 wisely
* Plan → Analyze market, companies, trends
* Execute → Gather and process data
* Result → Provide investment advice

This demonstrates how Agentic AI:

* Thinks (plans)
* Acts (executes)
* Learns (adapts)

All with minimal human intervention.
