= Case Study: Agentic AI vs Traditional AI (Investing $100)
:toc: right

== Scenario

You have $100 and want to invest in the US stock market.

You ask:

"How should I invest this $100?"

Now let’s see how two different systems respond.

== Approach 1: Traditional AI

=== How It Works

Traditional AI is *task-based*.

It does only what you explicitly ask.

=== Interaction Flow

You → "Suggest some US stocks"

AI → Gives a list of stocks

You → "Analyze Apple stock"

AI → Gives analysis

You → "Compare with Tesla"

AI → Gives comparison

You → "Suggest allocation for $100"

AI → Gives allocation

=== Key Observation

* You are driving every step
* AI is only responding to instructions
* No independent thinking or planning

=== Summary

*Traditional AI waits for instructions.*

== Approach 2: Agentic AI

=== How It Works

Agentic AI is *goal-based*.

It focuses on the outcome, not just tasks.

=== Interaction Flow

You → "How should I invest $100?"

AI internally:

* Understands the goal
* Creates a plan
* Executes the plan

=== What AI Does Internally

* Identifies possible investment options
* Analyzes market conditions
* Evaluates companies
* Assesses risk vs return
* Decides allocation strategy

=== Final Output

AI → 

* Suggested stocks or ETFs
* Allocation of $100
* Explanation of decisions

=== Key Observation

* AI decides what steps are needed
* AI executes them automatically
* Minimal or no human intervention

=== Summary

*Agentic AI figures out what to do.*

== Side-by-Side Comparison

[cols="1,1", options="header"]
|===
| Traditional AI | Agentic AI

| Task-based
| Goal-based

| Needs step-by-step instructions
| Plans steps on its own

| Reactive
| Proactive

| Human-driven
| AI-driven

| No planning capability
| Has planning + execution capability
|===

== Final Insight

Both systems use AI, but the difference is in control:

* Traditional AI → You are the driver  
* Agentic AI → AI becomes the driver  

== One-Line Takeaway

*Traditional AI helps you do tasks.*  
*Agentic AI helps you achieve goals.*
