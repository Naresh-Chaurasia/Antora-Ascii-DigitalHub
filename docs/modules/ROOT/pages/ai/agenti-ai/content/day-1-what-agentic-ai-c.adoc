= Day 1 – What is Agentic AI?
:toc: right


Agentic AI consists of autonomous systems that proactively solve problems by reasoning, making decisions, and using tools to achieve goals

== Learning Objectives

** Understand what defines an AI agent
** Explain how agentic AI differs from traditional systems
** Identify key capabilities of AI agents
** Understand multi-agent systems and their benefits
** Recognize when and why frameworks are used

== What is Agentic AI


** Autonomous systems
** Make decisions and take actions independently
** Goal-oriented behavior
** Work towards a defined objective
** Proactive nature
** Do not wait for instructions, take initiative

* *Core Capabilities of AI Agents*

** Multi-step reasoning
*** Break complex problems into smaller steps
*** Example: solving a research problem step by step

** *Decision-making*
*** Evaluate options and choose best action

** *Tool usage*
*** Call APIs, external tools, or other agents
*** Example: fetching data from a database

** *Memory and context*
*** Retain past interactions
*** Improve continuity and accuracy over time

---

* *Reactive vs Agentic Systems*

** *Reactive systems*
*** Respond only when input is given
*** Example: calculator
** *Agentic systems*
*** Understand goals and act continuously
*** Example: research assistant completing a task end-to-end

---

* *Role of Frameworks*
** Why not build from scratch
** Complex infrastructure required
* What frameworks provide
** Memory management
** Tool integration
** Agent coordination
** Analogy: Frameworks act like rails guiding development

---

* *Multi-Agent Systems*
** Multiple agents working together
** Similar to a software development team
** Each agent has a specific role

* *Benefits*
** Specialization
** Each agent focuses on one task
** Parallel processing
** Multiple agents work at the same time
** Fault tolerance
** System continues even if one agent fails
** Scalability
** Add more agents as complexity increases
** Modularity
** Easily update or replace individual agents

---

*Quick Revision Points*

** AI agents are autonomous and goal-oriented
** They perform reasoning, decision-making, and tool usage
** Agentic AI is proactive, unlike reactive systems
** Frameworks simplify building agent systems
** Multi-agent systems improve efficiency through collaboration
** Benefits include specialization, scalability, and modularity
