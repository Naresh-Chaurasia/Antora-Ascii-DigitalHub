** Planning actions

*** Operates independently with little or no human intervention
** Goal-based behavior

** Traditional AI is task-based
*** Performs predefined tasks
** Agentic AI is goal-driven
*** Decides actions dynamically to achieve a goal

=== 3. Traditional AI vs Agentic AI

> Not Used.

.Traditional AI is task-focused, while Agentic AI is goal-driven and autonomous in decision-making
[%collapsible]
====
* Traditional AI is task-focused, while Agentic AI is goal-driven and autonomous in decision-making

* Real-world example: self-driving cars
** Both systems can be used but behave differently
** Differences are clear in goal, adaptability, and autonomy

* Difference in goals
** Traditional AI
*** Focuses on a specific task like object detection
*** Identifies objects (e.g., person or obstacle on road)
*** Alerts driver based on predefined rules
** Agentic AI
*** Focuses on a broader goal like route navigation
*** Considers traffic, weather, obstacles, and external data
*** Makes decisions to reach destination efficiently

* Difference in adaptability
** Traditional AI
*** Limited to training data
*** Fails to recognize unseen objects
*** Does not learn in real time
** Agentic AI
*** Learns from new situations and past experiences
*** Adapts to unexpected conditions
*** Continuously improves decision-making

* Difference in autonomy
** Traditional AI
*** Executes predefined instructions
*** Example: detect object → send alert
*** Requires human intervention in many cases
** Agentic AI
*** Plans and decides next actions independently
*** Gathers additional data (traffic, weather, APIs)
*** Takes dynamic decisions to achieve the goal

=== 13,14. Multi Agent Architecture

*CrewAI: Chain of Thoughts and Step-by-Step Execution*

*Overview*

*CrewAI orchestrates AI agents using Chain of Thoughts (CoT) - a sequential reasoning approach where agents think step-by-step before acting.*

*Core Architecture*

* *1. Flows* - Orchestration layer (Process Manager)
* *2. #Crews#* - Collaboration layer (Team Management)  
* *3. Agents* - Execution layer (Task Performers)

*Two Execution Approaches*

*Simple (Your Original):*
*Crew starts directly with crew.kickoff()*
*No orchestration layer*
*Good for single workflow*

[source,python]
----
# Direct Crew execution - no Flow layer
crew = Crew(agents=[researcher, writer], tasks=[task1, task2])
result = crew.kickoff()  # Crew starts directly
----

*Advanced (With Flow):*

*Flow starts first with flow.start()*
*Flow manages multiple Crews/steps*
*Better for complex applications*

[source,python]
----
# Flow orchestrates multiple Crews
flow = Flow()
flow.add_step(crew_execution_step)
result = flow.start()  # Flow starts, then calls Crew
----

*Original code works perfectly without Flow* - Flow is optional and used when you need to orchestrate multiple workflows or add state management between different Crew executions.

*Chain of Thoughts Process*

Agents break down tasks into logical steps:

1. *Analyze* - Understand the problem
2. *Plan* - Create step-by-step approach
3. *Execute* - Perform each step sequentially
4. *Validate* - Check results before proceeding

*Step-by-Step Execution Flow*

```
Flow Starts → Crew Assembles → Agents Execute (CoT) → Results Integrate → Flow Continues
```

*Agent Execution Example:*

- "I need to analyze data. First, I'll examine the structure..."
- "Data loaded. Next, I'll identify patterns..."
- "Patterns found. Finally, I'll generate insights..."

=== 15,16 Designing Specialized Agents for Customer Support

*Overview*

A multi-agent system that creates and analyzes recipes for diabetic patients using CrewAI.

*How It Works*

* *Two agents work together:*
* 1. Chef Agent - Creates 5 different recipes
* 2. Nutritionist Agent - Analyzes and selects the best recipe for diabetics

*Process Flow*

* Input Ingredients → Chef Creates Recipes → Nutritionist Analyzes → Final Recommendation

*Agent Roles*

*Chef Agent:*

- Creates 5 recipes using given ingredients
- Includes cooking steps and nutritional info
- Considers taste and presentation

*Nutritionist Agent:*

- Reviews all recipes for diabetic suitability
- Selects the best recipe for diabetic patients
- Explains why other recipes are not suitable

*Code Example*

```python
# Create agents
chef = Agent(role="chef", goal="Create recipes using {ingredients}")
nutritionist = Agent(role="nutritionist", goal="Analyze recipes for diabetics")

# Create tasks
cook_task = Task(description="Create 5 recipes using {ingredients}", agent=chef)
analyze_task = Task(description="Select best recipe for diabetics", agent=nutritionist)

# Create crew and run
crew = Crew(agents=[chef, nutritionist], tasks=[cook_task, analyze_task])
result = crew.kickoff(inputs={"ingredients": "chicken"})
```

*What You Get*

*A complete analysis including:*
- Selected recipe with cooking instructions
- Why it's good for diabetic patients
- Reasons why other recipes were rejected
- Final recommendation from nutritionist

*Key Benefits*

- *Specialized expertise* - Chef and nutritionist knowledge
- *Quality control* - Medical validation of recipes
- *Patient safety* - Evidence-based recommendations
- *Easy to use* - Just provide ingredients

*Use Cases*

- Healthcare providers creating meal plans
- Nutrition apps for diabetic patients
- Educational tools for healthy cooking
- Personal meal planning services

*Technical Details*

- Uses GPT-4 for smart reasoning
- Sequential processing (chef first, then nutritionist)
- Detailed logging for transparency
- Context preservation between tasks

*Conclusion*

This system shows how AI agents can collaborate to solve real-world health problems. The chef creates options, and the nutritionist ensures medical safety - perfect for diabetic meal planning.

=== 24. Phidata Implementation: Constructing the Team

A multi-agent investment analysis system that combines market sentiment analysis, financial data evaluation, and strategic investment recommendations using specialized AI agents.

*System Architecture*

*Agent Components:*

* 1. Market Sentiment Evaluator - Analyzes financial news sentiment
* 2. Financial Insights Agent - Extracts key financial metrics
* 3. Deep Financial Analytics Agent - Comprehensive financial analysis
* 4. Strategic Investment Advisor - Makes final investment decisions
* 5. Investment Analysis Team - Coordinates all agents*

*Implementation Details*

*Core Dependencies:*
```python
from phi.agent import Agent
from phi.model.openai import OpenAIChat
from phi.tools.yfinance import YFinanceTools
from phi.tools.googlesearch import GoogleSearch
from phi.tools.calculator import Calculator
```

*Agent Configuration:*

*Market Sentiment Bot:*
```python
market_sentiment_bot = Agent(
    name="Market Sentiment Evaluator",
    role="Analyze and interpret financial news articles",
    model=OpenAIChat(id="gpt-4o"),
    tools=[GoogleSearch(), YFinanceTools(company_news=True)],
    instructions=[
        "Identify relevant news articles and assess sentiment",
        "Assign sentiment ratings from 1 (very negative) to 10 (very positive)",
        "Provide specific sources and detailed reasoning"
    ]
)
```

*Financial Data Bot:*
```python
financial_data_bot = Agent(
    name="Financial Insights Agent",
    role="Extract and analyze company financial data",
    model=OpenAIChat(id="gpt-4o"),
    tools=[YFinanceTools(stock_price=True, company_info=True, stock_fundamentals=True)],
    instructions=[
        "Gather stock prices, analyst ratings, and key metrics",
        "Structure data in tables with meaningful insights"
    ]
)
```

*Deep Analytics Bot:*
```python
deep_financials_bot = Agent(
    name="Deep Financial Analytics Agent",
    role="Extract extensive company financial data",
    model=OpenAIChat(id="gpt-4o"),
    tools=[YFinanceTools(company_info=True, key_financial_ratios=True, income_statements=True)],
    instructions=[
        "Gather financial ratios, income statements, balance sheets",
        "Highlight company fundamentals and trends"
    ]
)
```

*Investment Strategy Bot:*
```python
investment_strategy_bot = Agent(
    name="Strategic Investment Advisor",
    role="Make data-driven investment decisions",
    model=OpenAIChat(id="gpt-4o"),
    instructions=[
        "Verify outputs for accuracy and completeness",
        "Analyze data holistically for final sentiment score",
        "Provide clear BUY, SELL, or HOLD recommendation"
    ]
)
```

*Team Coordination:*
```python
investment_analysis_team = Agent(
    model=OpenAIChat(id="gpt-4o"),
    team=[market_sentiment_bot, financial_data_bot, deep_financials_bot, investment_strategy_bot],
    instructions=[
        "Integrate insights from all specialized agents",
        "Structure findings in clear tables",
        "Provide definitive investment recommendation"

```
