= Productivity Simplified: Achieve Goals With Minimal Effort

> Goal Setting

== Introduction

Welcome to this goal setting guide. This is a step-by-step system to:
- Review the past (year / quarter / month)
- Identify patterns
- Set meaningful goals

*What you'll need:*

- Access to your calendar
- A few hours of uninterrupted time
- Honest reflection

##############################################

---

== Part 1: Reflection (Looking Back)

- Set up your environment for focused work
- Take a few deep breaths
- Start without overthinking

---

=== Task A: Calendar Review

Go through your calendar week by week.

For each event:

- Write the event
- Mark it as *Win* or *Loss*
- Leave space for reflection

Use life areas (health, career, relationships, etc.) if needed.

=== Task C: Unfinished Sentences

Complete these within 5–10 minutes:

- The biggest risk I took this year was…
- The wisest decision I made was…
- The biggest lesson I learnt was…
- The biggest surprise of the year was…
- The three people who influenced me the most were…
- The biggest thing I completed was…
- The best thing I discovered about myself is…
- My best moments of the year were…

---

=== Task D: Key Questions

==== Major Milestones
What were the 3–5 most significant events? How did they impact your life?

==== Challenges Overcome
What obstacles did you face? What did you learn?

==== Personal Growth
How have you changed in beliefs, behavior, or mindset?

==== Self Care & Well-being
What habits improved or declined?

==== Relationships
How did your relationships evolve?

==== Professional / Academic Growth
What did you learn and achieve?

==== Gratitude
What are you most grateful for?

==== Forgiveness
What needs to be forgiven (others or yourself)?

== Part 2: Reflection (Looking Forward)

=== Task A: What Will the Next Year Be About?

- This year I will spend more time…

- This year will be the year of…
